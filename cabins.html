Otono One Availability

Otono One is only installed on selected aircraft. It is extremely limited, especially on the Boeing 777-300ER and Boeing 787-9 where only two suites are installed.

Aircraft with Otono One

✅ Airbus A380-800: 4 suites only
✅ Boeing 777-300ER: selected aircraft, 2 suites only
✅ Boeing 787-9: selected aircraft, 2 suites only
✅ Airbus A350-1000ULR: 4 suites only

Aircraft without Otono One

❌ Airbus A320 family
❌ Airbus A350-900
❌ Boeing 787-8
❌ Boeing 777-200
❌ Airbus A340-500 in normal service
