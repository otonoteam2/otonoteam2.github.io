# Player Time Rank Score

Formula

Score = Time² × Difficulty × Hint Modifier × Area Modifier × Finder Modifier

Hint Modifier = 1 ÷ (1 + 0.14 × hints)
Area Modifier = 1 + (0.04 × area level)
Finder Modifier = 1 + (0.03 × extra finders)

This means time matters most, but hints, area, and finders still affect the final score.
