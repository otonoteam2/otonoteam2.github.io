Formula Display

Score = T⁴ × H × A × I × S × R × D

T = total time in seconds, including milliseconds
H = 30 ÷ (30 + (Hints × Hint Strength × 2))
A = 0.75 + (0.18 × Area Rating)
I = 1 + (0.08 × Items Hidden)
S = 1 + (0.18 × Finders)
R = Average Group Age ÷ Player Age, capped between 0.75 and 1.75
D = Difficulty Mode Multiplier

If age is 0, the age multiplier is temporarily treated as ×1 so the calculator can start from 0 safely.
