‚ùì FAQs

What if you score 65?
No rounding. 66 is Pass. 65 stays 65. The response to ‚Äúpass is high‚Äù was tiers and easier Foundation papers ‚Äî not moving the number.
Why is 100 so rare, and why do perfect scripts often get 99?
100 is treated as special. Many scripts that are fully correct may still receive 99 because markers reserve 100 for answers that are not just correct, but also exceptionally clear and complete.
What pen or pencil do you have to use?
Anything is allowed as long as it is legible. Otono will mark it.
What if the paper says ‚Äúdon‚Äôt use black ink‚Äù and you use it anyway?
Your work is still marked, but you may receive an ink-rule penalty. The maximum penalty is 50% of the mark. The script is never confiscated.
