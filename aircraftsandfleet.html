Active Otono Airways Fleet

These aircraft form the main Otono Airways long-haul fleet. This section links to the real aircraft pages only.

Flagship aircraft

Airbus A380

The Airbus A380-800 is Otono Airways' flagship superjumbo, used for major long-haul routes and high-demand services. Otono Airways originally had 27 A380-800s: 19 remain active, 3 are archived, and 5 were sold to Emirates. Known for Otono One, First, Business, Premium Economy, Economy, lounges and showers.
